About Exhibit

Exhibit 2024 is an Inter Collegiate university level competition organized by Physics, Mathematics and Statistics Departments, Nalanda Degree College. The event consists of PowerPoint Presentation, Poster Presentation and Quiz and also Informal Events. The objective of Exhibit is to create a platform for Degree students to exhibit their knowledge and skills.

Rules

• The programme starts at 9 a.m. on 19th March, 2024.
• Participants have to submit bonafied letter from their respective colleges at the time of registration.
• Judges and organizers decision will be final.
• Working lunch will be provided.
• Participants are requested to send registration form by hand on or before 19-03-2024 (offline only.)
• Spot registration will be accepted
• Registration fee for each participant is Rs.100/- for 2 Events. (Additional each Event 50/-)
• From each college 3 PPT’s are accepted from each department.
• Maximum time for each PPT should not exceeded 10 min.
• All Degree students are eligible
• Each Quiz Team consists of 3 members only
